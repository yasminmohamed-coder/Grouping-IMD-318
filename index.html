About Sustainability

In PROTON, we focus on environmental, social, and governance (ESG) principles to ensure long-term impact.


Who Are We?


PROTON was founded in 1983, PROTON is Malaysia's national automotive company. Today, sustainability has become one of the company's core priorities. Through innovation, environmental protection and responsible governance, PROTON strives to create long-term value for customers, employees, communities and the nation.
